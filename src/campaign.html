Creating A New Earth Conscious Lifestyle Together with LG

Throwaway fashion is putting pressure on our planet. 5.8 million tonnes of post-consumer textile waste is generated every year in Europe alone. The most sustainable garment is the one you already own. The better you care for it, the longer it will last. In pursuit of the 2021 Sustainable Development Goals set out by the UN, LG Electronics’ Clothing Care Campaign aims to contribute by helping you care for your clothes sustainably.

Climate Resilience project

LG will make a donation of USD 100,000 to OXFAM, with plans to run a Climate Resilience project in Nepal. Nepal is facing increasing climate-related disasters such as floods, landslides anddroughts brough in part from excessive clothing production and consumption.
This project supports community-based organisations in one of the most vulnerable provinces in Nepal to improve knowledge of climate change, offer training on climate-smart livelihoods, and enhance the capability of local organisations to influence local decision-making and raise income.
